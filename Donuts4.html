Doughnuts have been popular for over a hundred years and have made their way into many cultures around the world.

Doughnuts come in many shapes, sizes, and decorations.

The most common doughnut varieties found in the United States are:

  • Glazed
  • Crullers
  • Apple Fritters
  • Creme or jelly filled
  • Maple bars--with or without bacon
  • Doughnut holes
